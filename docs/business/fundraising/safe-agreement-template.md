## 1. DEFINITIONS

**1.1** "**Capital Stock**" means the share capital of the Company, including without limitation all classes of common shares and preferred shares.

**1.2** "**Conversion Shares**" means shares of the Company's Capital Stock issued to the Investor upon conversion of this SAFE pursuant to Section 2.

**1.3** "**Discount Rate**" means 80% (representing a 20% discount).

**1.4** "**Dissolution Event**" means (i) a voluntary termination of operations of the Company, (ii) a general assignment for the benefit of the Company's creditors, or (iii) any other liquidation, dissolution, or winding up of the Company, whether voluntary or involuntary.

**1.5** "**Equity Financing**" means a bona fide transaction or series of transactions with the principal purpose of raising capital, pursuant to which the Company issues and sells its preferred shares (or equivalent equity securities) at a fixed pre-money valuation, resulting in aggregate gross proceeds to the Company of not less than CAD **$500,000** (excluding the conversion of SAFEs or other convertible instruments) (the "**Minimum Qualifying Financing**").

**1.6** "**Liquidity Event**" means (i) a Change of Control, or (ii) an initial public offering of the Company's shares on a recognized stock exchange. A "**Change of Control**" means (a) a transaction or series of related transactions in which any person or group of related persons acquires more than 50% of the outstanding voting shares of the Company, or (b) a sale, lease, or other disposition of all or substantially all of the assets of the Company.

**1.7** "**Maturity Date**" means the date that is **24 months** from the Effective Date.

**1.8** "**MFN Amendment Provision**" has the meaning set forth in Section 5.

**1.9** "**Valuation Cap**" means CAD **$2,000,000** (pre-money).

## 2. CONVERSION EVENTS

### 2.1 Equity Financing

Upon the closing of an Equity Financing, this SAFE will automatically convert into Conversion Shares at a price per share equal to the **lesser of**:

**(a)** the price per share equal to the **Valuation Cap** divided by the number of shares of Capital Stock outstanding immediately prior to the Equity Financing (on a fully diluted basis, including the shares reserved for issuance under any equity incentive plan but excluding the conversion of this SAFE and any other SAFEs); or

**(b)** the price per share paid by the investors in the Equity Financing **multiplied by the Discount Rate** (i.e., at a 20% discount to the round price).

The number of Conversion Shares shall be determined by dividing the Investment Amount by the applicable price per share determined above. The Conversion Shares shall have the same rights, preferences, privileges, and restrictions as the shares issued in the Equity Financing.

### 2.2 Liquidity Event

If a Liquidity Event occurs before an Equity Financing, the Investor shall, at the Investor's option, receive either:

**(a)** a cash payment equal to the Investment Amount; or

**(b)** conversion of the Investment Amount into shares of the Company's Capital Stock at a price per share equal to the Valuation Cap divided by the number of shares of Capital Stock outstanding immediately prior to the Liquidity Event (on a fully diluted basis).

### 2.3 Dissolution Event

If a Dissolution Event occurs before an Equity Financing or Liquidity Event, the Company shall pay to the Investor an amount equal to the Investment Amount, prior to any distribution to holders of Capital Stock. If the Company's assets are insufficient to pay all SAFE holders in full, the available assets shall be distributed to SAFE holders on a pro rata basis according to their respective Investment Amounts.

### 2.4 Maturity

If neither an Equity Financing, Liquidity Event, nor Dissolution Event has occurred by the Maturity Date, then at the election of the holders of a majority-in-interest of the outstanding SAFEs (by Investment Amount):

**(a)** the Maturity Date shall be extended by an additional **12-month period** (which extension may occur only once); or

**(b)** the SAFE shall convert into shares of the Company's most senior class of Capital Stock at a price per share equal to the Valuation Cap divided by the number of shares of Capital Stock outstanding on the Maturity Date (on a fully diluted basis).

For clarity, the Investor has no right to demand repayment of the Investment Amount at the Maturity Date, except in the case of a Dissolution Event under Section 2.3.

## 5. MFN AMENDMENT PROVISION

If the Company issues any subsequent SAFEs with terms more favourable to the investors thereunder than the terms of this SAFE (including, without limitation, a lower Valuation Cap or a higher Discount Rate), then upon written notice from the Company, this SAFE shall be automatically amended to incorporate such more favourable terms, at the Investor's election. The Company shall promptly notify the Investor of any such issuance.

## 6. INFORMATION RIGHTS

The Company shall provide the Investor with an annual summary letter, within 120 days of the end of each fiscal year, containing: (i) a brief narrative update on the Company's business progress; (ii) a high-level summary of the Company's financial position; and (iii) any material events affecting the Company's business. The Investor shall have no right to inspect the Company's books and records, attend board meetings, or receive board observer rights.
